Lasagna

This is a brief summary of a lasagna recipe

Ingredients
  • Ground Beef, 1lb.
  • Italian Sausage, 1lb.
  • One Onion, diced
  • Can of Tomatos, crushed, 160z.
  • Minced Garlic. SO much garlic!
  • Fresh rosemary
  • Fresh basil
  • Ricotta cheese, 1lb
  • Fresh mozzerella, 1lb
  1. Brown the beef, drain
  2. Brown the sausage, drain
  3. In a large pot, combine beef, sausage, onions, garlic, herbs and tomatos
  4. Simmer for quite some time
  5. In a seperate pot, par boil lasagna noodles
  6. In a large rectangular pan, layer meat sauce, noodles, ricotta cheese and mozzerella cheese
  7. Cover with aluminun foil and bake for at least one hour
  8. Uncover and bake for an additional 20+ minutes, or until cheese around the edges is golden brown

Serve to your amazed friends and family with wine and bread. Salad optional
